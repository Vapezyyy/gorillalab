Privacy & Policy - Gorilla Lab

Privacy & Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Gorilla Lab collects only essential data required for gameplay and community engagement.

Information we collect includes:

  • Your IP address (used for analytics and multiplayer integrity)
  • Your Meta Display Name (used to identify players in multiplayer)

We do not collect personal identifying information such as your email, physical address, or payment details. All data is securely stored and never shared with third parties. By playing Gorilla Lab, you consent to this data policy in accordance with platform requirements.

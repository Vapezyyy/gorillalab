Gorilla Lab

Immersive VR Sandbox Game with Gorilla-Style Hand Movement

Enter the Lab, Become the Gorilla

Gorilla Lab is a cutting-edge VR sandbox game where you move like a real gorilla! Climb, swing, and explore with full hand-based locomotion in a physics-driven environment. Experiment, build, and go bananas in a lab built for chaos and creativity.

Game Features:

  • True VR locomotion with two-hand gorilla movement
  • Interactive sandbox with dynamic physics
  • Multiplayer chaos & co-op gameplay
  • Modding support for custom content
  • Stylized lab environments with secrets to discover
